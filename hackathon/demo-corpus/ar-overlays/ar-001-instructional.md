Step 4 of 9 · Replace condenser fan motor

You're looking at the condenser fan housing. The motor is the
cylindrical component on the right side of the fan blade.

Tools needed (visible in your tray):

- 5/16" nut driver
- Multimeter
- Replacement motor (part #HC39GE242, in your kit)

Estimated time: 18 minutes for an experienced technician.

Sequence:

1. Confirm power is off at the disconnect (red LED on the unit
   should be dark)
2. Remove the four bolts on the fan grille (highlighted in green)
3. Disconnect the three motor leads — note the wire colors before
   you remove them
4. Lift the motor straight out

extractor to your next site visit kit. Manager approval is automatic
