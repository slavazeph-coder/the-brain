Subject: Important update from the leadership team — please read in
full

Team,

I want to start by saying that I love this company. Every one of you
makes Meridian what it is, and the work you've done over the last 18
months has been nothing short of historic.

That's why today's decisions are so hard, but ultimately so right.

Effective Monday, June 16, we are ending our remote-first
configuration and returning to a five-day in-office work week at all
US locations. This is the right move for our culture, for our
customers, and most importantly for the kind of company we want to be
in 2026 and beyond. The data is clear: high-performing teams build
high-performing products in the same room. The collaboration energy
we've been missing simply cannot be replicated over Zoom, no matter
how good the technology gets.

We are also, with sadness, parting ways with approximately 14% of our
workforce. These were not performance decisions. These were strategic
decisions about where Meridian needs to invest its energy as we move
into our next chapter. Affected colleagues will be notified by their
manager within the next 90 minutes and will receive a generous
transition package.

I know change is hard. I know some of you will be frustrated. That
frustration is valid, and the leadership team welcomes it — please
share it with your manager, who is fully aligned and ready to help
you process this.

I'm asking each of you to lean in. The colleagues who choose Meridian's
next chapter with us are the ones who will define it. Together we will
emerge from this stronger than we have ever been.

With gratitude,
Sarah Chen
Founder & CEO, Meridian
